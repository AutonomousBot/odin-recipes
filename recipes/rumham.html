Sailor Jerry x Dietz & Watson Rum Ham

Description

The company of IASIP decided to celebrate the return of It’s Always Sunny by teaming up with Dietz & Watson
to come up with a recipe for rum ham that doesn’t taste like sadness and regret.

Ingredients

  • 1 Dietz & Watson Tiffany Dinner Ham
  • 3 ½ parts of Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum
  • 1 part pineapple juice
  • 1 part light brown sugar
  • ¼ parts salt
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 3 – 5 parts water
  • Pineapple slices and maraschino cherry for garnish

Steps

  1. Add 3 parts of Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum & pineapple juice to a large stockpot.
  2. Add peppercorns and cloves.
  3. Bring to a boil. Let boil for 3-5 minutes. Reduce heat to a simmer.
  4. Add brown sugar and salt. Whisk until sugar is dissolved.
  5. Add ham to the pot and add enough water to cover the ham.
    Let simmer for 25 minutes or until ham is warmed through. Turn off heat.
  6. Add remaining ½ part of Sailor Jerry Spiced Rum and let sit in liquid for 10 additional minutes.
  7. Remove ham from pot, top with pineapple slices and cherry and serve.
    Use reserved liquid to pour on top of slices.
